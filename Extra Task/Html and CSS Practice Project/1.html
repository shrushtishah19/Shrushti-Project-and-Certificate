Capgemini

About Company

Capgemini is a French multinational company situated in Paris,France.
The company is an EUR 12.5 billion( revenue FY 2016) global market
leader in the field of business consulting, technology and outsourcing
services. Capgemini helps its clients by developing their business with
the help of technology and digital solutions that fit their needs, thus
enabling them to achieve competitiveness and innovation. Capgemini
has over 200,000 employees in over 40 countries, of whom 100,000
are in India. Capgemini was founded by Serge Kampf in the year 1967,
as a data processing company. The company was initially inaugurated
as Sogeti.Paul Hermelin is the CEO and Chairman of Capgemini.
Capgemini helps business and organizations to build transformational
programs that will help their clients to improve growth and sharpen
their competitive edge over other organizations.
